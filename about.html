About the clinic

Expert care, rooted in 24 years of experience

Ankita's Care Clinic was founded on a simple belief: with the right preparation and support, every woman can approach birth with confidence, joy and a deep sense of achievement.

Dr. Ankita Panchal

Senior Physiotherapist & Women's Wellness Specialist

Dr. Ankita Panchal is a qualified and senior Physical Therapist with more than 24 years of experience. She has worked as a Senior Consultant Physical Therapist at Shalby Hospital.

After completing her Master's in Hospital Administration, she served as in-charge of Vijay Shalby Hospital. She then began working for the fitness of women of all age groups — from adolescent girls to women of menopausal age.

She also provides Corporate Training in Ergonomics, Fitness, Nutrition and Lifestyle Management, and has trained many rural and urban adolescent children and women.

After taking special training in Antenatal and Postnatal Care, she began training pregnant and post-pregnant women. She believes that the right information and preparation during pregnancy instils the confidence in a woman to birth her baby with an intense feeling of joy, satisfaction and achievement.
