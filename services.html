Our programs

Care for every stage of your pregnancy

Thoughtfully designed programs that combine physiotherapy, gentle movement and mindful preparation — delivered in supportive small batches or personal one-on-one sessions.

Movement & strength

Pre-Natal Exercises & Yoga

Safe, expertly guided movement designed for each trimester — easing the everyday discomforts of pregnancy while building the strength and stamina that support an easier labour and recovery.

  • Trimester-appropriate exercises supervised by a physiotherapist
  • Gentle prenatal yoga, stretching and breathing techniques
  • Posture and back-care guidance to relieve pregnancy aches
  • Pelvic floor and breathing work to prepare for labour
Knowledge & confidence

Pre-Natal Information

Clear, reliable guidance that replaces uncertainty with confidence — so you understand what's happening in your body and how to care for yourself and your baby through every trimester.

  • Nutrition and lifestyle guidance for a healthy pregnancy
  • What to expect in each trimester, explained simply
  • Preparation for labour, delivery and the early days with baby
  • Postnatal recovery and self-care advice
Bond & wellbeing

Garbh Sanskar

A time-honoured Indian practice of nurturing your baby in the womb. Through music, meditation, positive thinking and mindful routines, Garbh Sanskar nourishes both mother and child and deepens the bond between you.

  • Guided meditation, music and relaxation for mother and baby
  • Practices to encourage positivity and emotional wellbeing
  • Mindful daily routines that strengthen the mother–baby bond
