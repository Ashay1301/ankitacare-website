Our programs

Care for every stage of your pregnancy

Thoughtfully designed programs that combine physiotherapy, gentle movement, mindful preparation, and pelvic health care — personalised to every stage of your journey.

Knowledge & confidence

Pre-Natal Information

Clear, reliable guidance that replaces uncertainty with confidence — so you understand what's happening in your body and how to care for yourself and your baby through every trimester.

  • Nutrition and lifestyle guidance for a healthy pregnancy
  • What to expect in each trimester, explained simply
  • Preparation for labour, delivery and the early days with baby
  • Postnatal recovery and self-care advice
What's covered in detail

Knowledge sessions include:

  • •Importance of exercise and posture awareness during pregnancy
  • •Understanding Normal Labour and how to make it easier
  • •Caesarean section — what to expect and how to prepare
  • •Managing common pregnancy discomforts with confidence
  • •Post-delivery care and recovery for the new mother
  • •Breastfeeding guidance and support
  • •New-born baby care — understanding your baby's needs
Movement & strength

Pre-Natal Exercises & Yoga

Safe, expertly guided movement designed for each trimester — easing the everyday discomforts of pregnancy while building the strength and stamina that support an easier labour and recovery.

  • Trimester-appropriate exercises supervised by a physiotherapist
  • Gentle prenatal yoga, stretching and breathing techniques
  • Posture and back-care guidance to relieve pregnancy aches
  • Pelvic floor and breathing work to prepare for labour
What's covered in detail

Fitness sessions include:

  • •Program begins after 14 weeks of pregnancy (on gynaecologist's advice)
  • •Yoga and Aerobics exercises tailored for each trimester
  • •Special Pelvic floor exercises to strengthen core muscles
  • •Abdominal exercises adapted for pregnancy
  • •Labour exercises to build stamina for delivery
  • •Lamaze breathing technique — a proven pain-management method for labour
Bond & wellbeing

Garbh Sanskar

A time-honoured Indian practice of nurturing your baby in the womb. Through music, meditation, positive thinking and mindful routines, Garbh Sanskar nourishes both mother and child and deepens the bond between you.

  • Guided meditation, music and relaxation for mother and baby
  • Practices to encourage positivity and emotional wellbeing
  • Mindful daily routines that strengthen the mother–baby bond
What's covered in detail

Spiritual sessions include:

  • •Guided meditation to keep the mother calm and happy throughout pregnancy
  • •Music therapy — soothing sounds to stimulate and bond with your baby
  • •Positive affirmations and mindset practices for emotional strength
  • •Mindful daily routines rooted in traditional Indian wisdom
  • •Special Garbh Sanskar sessions available on request
Women's health care

Pelvic Health Physiotherapy

Helping Women Move, Recover, and Thrive Through Every Stage of Life — From Adolescence to Motherhood, Menopause, and Beyond.

Pregnancy Care

Supporting women through pregnancy with management of pelvic pain, back pain, pelvic floor dysfunction, and preparation for labour and delivery.

Postpartum Recovery

Helping mothers regain strength, recover from vaginal or C-section births, manage diastasis recti, pelvic floor weakness, and safely return to daily activities.

Pelvic Floor Rehabilitation

Assessment and treatment of urinary leakage, urgency, prolapse symptoms, bowel dysfunction, and pelvic floor muscle weakness or tightness.

Pelvic Pain Management

Evidence-based treatment for chronic pelvic pain, tailbone pain, painful bladder symptoms, and muscle-related pelvic discomfort.

Sexual Health Physiotherapy

Helping women overcome pain during intimacy, vaginismus, pelvic floor tension, and conditions affecting sexual wellness and quality of life.

Menopause & Midlife Wellness

Managing bladder control issues, pelvic floor changes, prolapse symptoms, and maintaining strength and confidence during menopause.

Core & Functional Rehabilitation

Restoring core strength, posture, breathing mechanics, and movement patterns to improve function, fitness, and overall well-being.

Bladder & Bowel Health

Individualised care for constipation, bowel control concerns, overactive bladder, urinary frequency, and healthy bladder habits.

Pelvic Health Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of pelvic floor function, posture, breathing, movement patterns, and lifestyle factors to create a personalised treatment plan.
