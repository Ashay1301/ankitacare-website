Why it matters

Knowledge is the best preparation

Pregnancy brings rapid changes to your body — and very little formal guidance is given to help you understand them. Becoming a mother is a precious and challenging journey. A mother who understands what is happening in her body is able to manage discomforts, prepare for labour and recover with far greater confidence.

Session content

What we cover in our knowledge sessions

Each topic is explained simply and thoroughly, so nothing feels overwhelming.

Exercise & Posture

Why staying active matters during pregnancy, how to maintain good posture and relieve the aches that come with a growing bump.

Nutrition & Lifestyle

What to eat, what to avoid, and how to build daily habits that nourish both you and your baby through every trimester.

Trimester by Trimester

A clear, reassuring explanation of what happens in your body in each trimester — and how to care for yourself through each stage.

Normal Labour

Understanding the stages of normal labour, what to expect, and how to work with your body to make the experience as smooth as possible.

Caesarean Preparation

What a Caesarean section involves, how to prepare mentally and physically, and what recovery looks like so there are no surprises.

Breastfeeding & Newborn Care

Guidance on breastfeeding, understanding your newborn's needs and taking care of yourself in the early days and weeks after birth.

Beyond birth

Post-natal recovery & self-care

Ankita's Care also prepares you for life after delivery. Understanding post-natal recovery helps new mothers rebuild strength, manage the physical demands of a new baby, and care for themselves with the same attention they give their child.

  • Post-delivery exercises to aid recovery and reduce post-natal fat
  • Breastfeeding support and guidance
  • Understanding and caring for your newborn baby
  • Emotional wellbeing and self-care for the new mother
