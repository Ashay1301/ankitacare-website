What we offer

Programs designed around you and your baby

Every pregnancy is unique. Our programs blend physiotherapy, gentle movement and mindful preparation to support your body and your bond with your baby.

Pre-Natal Information

Clear, trustworthy guidance on nutrition, posture, what to expect each trimester, and preparing for birth — so you feel informed and in control.

Learn more

Pre-Natal Exercises & Yoga

Safe, guided movement and breathing to ease discomfort, build strength and prepare your body for an easier labour and recovery.

Learn more

Garbh Sanskar

A time-honoured practice of nurturing your baby in the womb through music, meditation and positivity — deepening the bond between mother and child.

Meet your guide

Dr. Ankita Panchal

A qualified, senior physiotherapist with more than 28 years of experience, Dr. Ankita served as a Senior Consultant Physical Therapist at Shalby Hospital and as in-charge of Vijay Shalby Hospital after completing her Master's in Hospital Administration.

With specialised training in antenatal and postnatal care, she helps pregnant and post-pregnant women approach birth with confidence — believing the right information and preparation lets a woman birth her baby with joy, satisfaction and achievement.
